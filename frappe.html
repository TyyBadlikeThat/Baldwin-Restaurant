Oreo Frappe

This delicious and refreshing frappe excellent for those hot summer days. These simple ingredents: a blender and a cup is all u need.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cup of ice
  • 1 Cup milk
  • 2 Tablespoon chocolate syrup
  • 2 Scoops ice cream
  • Whipped cream
  • Oreo cookie

Method:

  1. Place all ingredient in a blender ( except the whip cream) and blend at high speed for 2 minutes.
  2. Serve in a cold cup
  3. Granish with whip cream and enjoy
