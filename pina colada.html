Classic Pina Colada

This Classic Pina Colada recipe is fruity and refreshing, with sweet pineapple, creamy coconut, and rum. It’s a fun tropical drink, the perfect rum cocktail (or make it a mocktail) for summer!

The Magic Ingredients

  • Pineapple juice
  • Frozen pineapple chunks
  • Cream of coconut
  • Lime juice
  • White rum

Method:

  1. Here’s how to make this classic cocktail at home – it’s quick and easy!
  2. Blend the ingredients. Add everything except for the rum to a blender. Blend until smooth and thickened.
  3. Add the rum. Pour the rum into the blender and quickly blend to combine.
  4. Enjoy. Divide between two glasses and garnish as desired.
